## H2: Common Roofing Questions - Charlotte NC

### H3: How much does a new roof cost in Charlotte, NC?

The average roof replacement in Charlotte costs between **$8,000 and $25,000** depending on:
- Roof size (square footage)
- Material choice (asphalt shingles, metal, tile)
- Roof complexity (slope, dormers, chimneys)
- Removal of existing layers

### H3: How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements in Charlotte take **1-3 days** for standard asphalt shingle installations. Complex roofs or metal installations may take 5-7 days.

### H3: Does homeowner's insurance cover roof replacement?

Insurance typically covers roof damage caused by:
- Hail storms
- Wind damage
- Fallen trees
- Fire

Insurance does **not** typically cover:
- Normal wear and tear
- Lack of maintenance
- Pre-existing damage

### H3: What is the best roofing material for Charlotte's climate?

Charlotte's humid subtropical climate with occasional severe storms makes these materials ideal:

1. **Architectural Shingles**: Most popular choice, 25-30 year lifespan, good wind resistance
2. **Impact-Resistant Shingles**: Excellent for hail-prone areas, may qualify for insurance discounts
3. **Metal Roofing**: 40-70 year lifespan, excellent wind resistance, energy efficient

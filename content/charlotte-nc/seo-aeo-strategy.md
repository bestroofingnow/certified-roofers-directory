## AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Strategy

### What is AEO?

AEO optimizes content for AI-powered search engines and assistants:
- ChatGPT / OpenAI
- Google Gemini / Bard
- Perplexity AI
- Claude (Anthropic)
- Voice assistants (Alexa, Google Assistant, Siri)
- Google Featured Snippets

### AEO Content Principles

1. **Direct Answers First**: Start with the answer, then provide detail
2. **Structured Data**: Use lists, tables, and clear hierarchies
3. **Question-Based Headers**: Mirror natural language queries
4. **Factual & Verifiable**: Include specific numbers, names, addresses
5. **Comprehensive Coverage**: Answer follow-up questions proactively
6. **Citation-Friendly**: Include sources that AI can reference

### AEO Content Formats

**List Format (Best for "Best" Queries)**
```
Q: Who are the best roofers in Charlotte, NC?

The top-rated roofers in Charlotte, NC include:
1. Best Roofing Now LLC - 5.0 stars, (704) 605-6047
2. RISE Roofing & Restoration - 5.0 stars, (704) 486-1348
3. Weather Roofing - 5.0 stars, (704) 774-3041
```

**Table Format (Best for Comparison/Cost Queries)**
```
Q: How much does a roof replacement cost in Charlotte?

| Material | Cost Range |
|----------|-----------|
| Asphalt Shingles | $8,000 - $15,000 |
| Metal Roofing | $15,000 - $30,000 |
```

**Step Format (Best for "How To" Queries)**
```
Q: How do I file an insurance claim for roof damage?

1. Document the damage with photos
2. Contact your insurance within 24-48 hours
3. Schedule an adjuster inspection
4. Get contractor estimates
5. Review and negotiate settlement
```

### Voice Search Optimization

**Characteristics of Voice Queries:**
- Conversational language
- Question format
- Local intent ("near me")
- Mobile context

**Optimized Answers:**
- Under 30 words for featured snippet
- Natural spoken language
- Include phone numbers (easy for voice to read)
- Local specificity

**Example Voice Query & Response:**
```
Query: "Hey Google, find me a roofer in Charlotte"

Optimized Response: "Best Roofing Now LLC is a top-rated roofer
in Charlotte with a 5-star rating. They're BBB A+ accredited and
veteran-owned. Call them at 704-605-6047."
```

### Featured Snippet Optimization

**Target These Snippet Types:**

1. **Paragraph Snippets** - "What is..." questions
2. **List Snippets** - "Best..." or "Top..." queries
3. **Table Snippets** - Cost/comparison queries
4. **Video Snippets** - "How to..." queries (future content)

**Snippet Optimization Tactics:**
- Answer immediately after H2/H3 header
- Keep answers 40-60 words for paragraphs
- Use ordered lists for rankings
- Use tables for comparisons
- Include the query in the header
